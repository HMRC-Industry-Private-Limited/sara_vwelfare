PayTabs Payment Gateway Integration

This module facilitates users to execute payments seamlessly by utilizing the PayTabs Payment Acquirer, providing a convenient and efficient payment method.

  • Install the module from the apps menu.

  • To activate the PayTabs payment provider, navigate to the website, go to the "configuration" section, then select the "eCommerce" tab, and finally access the "payment providers" option.

  • Please configure the details such as PayTabs ID and Encryption Key to enable the use of the PayTabs provider.

  • After configuring the details, proceed to the "Website > Shop" section to select products and add them to the cart. Upon reaching the checkout process for the order, you will have the option to pay for the order using the PayTabs provider.

  • Select the preferred payment option to proceed with the order payment. Afterwards, click on the "Pay" button to initiate and complete the transaction.

  • When you click "Pay," it will take you to the PayTabs payment page where you can enter your payment details, such as your name, card number, and CVV. and complete the transaction.

  • After the payment is successful, You Will redirected to Odoo. Your order will be processed.

  • you will receive confirmation that the payment has been successfully processed.

  • Upon completion, the payment for your order will be successfully processed and confirmed, ensuring a seamless transaction through PayTabs..
